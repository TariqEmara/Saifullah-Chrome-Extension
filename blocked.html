KHALID IBN AL-WALID
سيف الله المسلول — The Drawn Sword of Allah
⚔ سيف الله ⚔
يَسْتَخْفُونَ مِنَ النَّاسِ وَلَا يَسْتَخْفُونَ مِنَ اللَّهِ وَهُوَ مَعَهُمْ إِذْ يُبَيِّتُونَ مَا لَا يَرْضَىٰ مِنَ الْقَوْلِ
"They hide from people but cannot hide from Allah — He is with them when they spend the night plotting what He does not approve of."
Surah An-Nisa 4:108 — سورة النساء
Allah forgave you — and this is how you act towards Him?
"O Khalid! You are the Sword of Allah. Allah has unsheathed you upon His enemies."
— The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, bestowing the title Saifullah upon Khalid ibn al-Walid (رضي الله عنه)
He wept on his deathbed, wishing he had died in battle. Not a hand-span of his body was free of a wound.

The Truthful — First Caliph of Islam

إِذْ هُمَا فِي الْغَارِ إِذْ يَقُولُ لِصَاحِبِهِ لَا تَحْزَنْ إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَنَا
"When they were in the cave — when he said to his companion: Do not grieve, indeed Allah is with us."
Surah At-Tawbah 9:40 — سورة التوبة
Allah forgave you — and this is how you act towards Him?
"When I accepted Islam, I never doubted for a single moment."
— Abu Bakr as-Siddiq (رضي الله عنه) — the only one who believed without hesitation
He sat beside the Prophet ﷺ in the cave, willing to die for him. The Quran recorded that moment for eternity.

The Prophet ﷺ heard Bilal's footsteps in Jannah before he even died. His voice broke the silence of Fajr forever.

He recited Quran once and the Prophet ﷺ stopped walking, stood still, listened — and his eyes filled with tears.

Allah forgave you —
and this is how you act towards Him?

He ﷺ was sent as a mercy to every soul — including yours.

He ﷺ alone will say: "Ummati, ummati — my people, my people."

He ﷺ said: "How I long to meet my brothers." The Sahabah asked: are we not your brothers? He ﷺ said: "You are my companions. My brothers are those who believed in me without ever seeing me."

He ﷺ wept for you. A stranger he would never meet in this life — and he stood before Allah, in tears, calling your name.

Don't you want to meet him? ﷺ
